This blog is intended as a home for **research-based analysis** on economic security, trade dependencies, energy security, and related questions.

The guiding principle is simple: **state the question clearly, use transparent methods, and keep claims proportional to the evidence**.

Future posts may include:

- replications of public methodologies
- exploratory analysis of trade and production data
- short notes on data sources and measurement choices
- longer essays on strategic exposure and resilience

A good first substantive post could be a replication of the **EU trade dependency methodology** for Norway, with a clear explanation of assumptions, product-level thresholds, and the limits of applying an EU framework to a non-EU case.
